What We Do

Learn

Pick up new skills and learn about the industry through workshops, talks, and activities. We welcome members of all interest and experience levels!

Connect

Meet fellow game-making enthusiasts and combine your individual skills! You don't need to be an extrovert, we're all looking to make new friends and build cool stuff.

Make Things

Participate in game jams, showcase your personal projects, practice your pitching skills, and more! We make digital games, table/board games, and non-games.
